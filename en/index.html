What is Vagari?

An intuitive digital platform with the best AI tools to create, manage and distribute audioguides adapted to your user groups – in minutes.

A friendly mobile web-app where your users can enjoy all your content.

How does it work?

📤

1. Upload

Upload relevant information of your space (photos, texts, voice notes…)

🎛️

2. Fine-tune & script

Select length, style, audience (general, kids 8-12…), languages and AI-generate the scripts

🔊

3. Voice & audio

Pick voices / narration styles and AI-generate the audio files

🚀

4. Publish

Review & publish to your visitor app – auto-generate QRs and paywalls if needed
